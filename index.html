Fujian Recipe: Bawn Mian (Peanut Butter Wonton Noodles)

Yield:

Serves: 1

Prep Time:

3 minutes

Cook Time:

10 minutes

In Fuzhou, province, the typical breakfeast food to start off the morning is bawn mian. The noodle is high in protein and carbohydrates and is a great way to feel energized and budget friendly!

Ingredients

  1. Package of wonton noodles
  2. Jar of peanut butter
  3. Soy sauce
  4. White vinegar
  5. (Optional) Green onions

Steps

  1. In a small pot fill it with water and bring
    it to a boil.
  2. While Waiting for the water to boil grab a
    bowl.
  3. In the bowl place the peanut butter, soy
    sauce and white vinegar.
  4. Once the water starts boiling, place the
    wonton noodles into the the pot.
  5. Cook the noodles for 3 to 5 minutes until
    it is slightly soft.
  6. Transfer the noodle from the pot to the
    bowl. Mix everything until the sauce
    becomes smooth. If it becomes too dry
    pour a bit of water from the pot and
    continue to mix until the sauce is
    smooth.
  7. Chop the green onions and toss it into
    the bowl.
